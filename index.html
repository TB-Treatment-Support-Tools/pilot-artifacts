Background

The TB Treatment Support Tools (TB-TSTs) are a set of software tools that help monitor adherence to TB medication regmines and provide support to patients throughout treatment.

This site describes the earliest set of the tools used in 2019 pilot study in Buenos Aires, Argentina.

Contributors

The code for the pilot study was primarily written by Grace Youngblood and Sean Campbell. Towards the end of the pilot Kyle Goodwin took over to fix issues encountered during the study and begin development of version 2 which is used in the ongoing RCT. Development was also supported by Ivan, Justin, and Bill of the Clincal Informatics Research Group at UW.
